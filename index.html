Titanic

In fair Southampton, where the great ships sail,
A young maiden named Rose did dwell.
She was of noble birth, but her heart was frail,
For she was betrothed to a man she did not love.

One day, on the maiden voyage of the Titanic,
Rose met a young artist named Jack.
He was poor and uncouth, but he was kind and gentle,
And he saw the beauty in Rose that no one else could see.

They fell in love, but their love was forbidden.
Rose's mother would never approve of Jack,
And the Titanic was a ship of class and privilege.

But Jack and Rose could not deny their love.
They snuck away together, and they found a place to be alone.
They kissed in the moonlight, and they made promises to each other.

But their happiness was not to last.
On the night of April 14, 1912, the Titanic struck an iceberg.
The ship began to sink, and chaos erupted.

Jack and Rose were separated in the crowd.
Jack tried to save Rose, but he was too late.
The Titanic sank, and Jack was lost.

Rose survived, but she was heartbroken.
She had lost the love of her life, and she would never be the same.

But she never forgot Jack.
She kept his memory alive in her heart,
And she knew that their love would never die.
